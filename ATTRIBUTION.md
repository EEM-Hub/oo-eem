## How Content Was Used

Source articles were fetched, converted to markdown, and summarized into structured entries. Factual beliefs were extracted from those summaries, then expanded through automated derivation, review, and repair cycles. The derived beliefs represent synthesized conclusions drawn from the source material, not direct quotations.
